Question presented

In New York State Bd. Of Elections v. Lopez Torres, 552 U.S. 196 (2008), this Court held: “a political party has a First Amendment right to limit its membership as it wishes, and to choose a candidate selection process that will in its view produce the nominee who best represents its political platform.
