Question presented

The questions presented in this petition are as follows: The first question presented is: Whether in a prosecution for speech under the “true threats” doctrine an objective standard of mere recklessness is sufficient to meet the scienter requirement. The second question presented is: Whether in a prosecution for speech under the “true threats” doctrine speech acts unknown to the person threatened shed any light on the state of mind of the party uttering the alleged threat.
