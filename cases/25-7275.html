Hira Uddin v. Texana Behavioral Healthcare & Development Services, dba Texana Center, et al.

IFP petition · Supreme Court of Texas, No. 25-0538 · judgment July 25, 2025

Pending

Questions presented

  1. Whether the Federal Arbitration Act, 9 U.S.C. § 2, and the Supremacy Clause prohibit a state court from compelling or enforcing arbitration after the opposing party has waived its right to arbitrate through litigation conduct inconsistent with an intent to arbitrate.

  2. Whether a state court violates the Due Process Clause of the Fourteenth Amendment by compelling arbitration without a showing of mutual assent and by disposing of preserved federal objections through an unreasoned denial that forecloses meaningful judicial review.
