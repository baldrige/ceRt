IFP petition · District Court of Appeal of Florida, Fourth District, No. 4D2023-3142 · judgment March 6, 2025

Certiorari denied · February 23, 2026

Questions presented

  1. In what circumstances is a search warrant invalid because it erroneously describes the place to be searched?

  2. Does the private party search doctrine apply where the “search” is made by a computer?

  3. Does a state court defendant charged with a felony have the right to trial by a twelve-member of jury under the Sixth Amendment?
