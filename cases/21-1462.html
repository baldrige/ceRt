Question presented

Whether a settlement in a case covered by statutory fee shifting provisions under terms entitling plaintiff’s attorneys to reasonable fees and costs as determined by a judge requires fees to be determined: a) under Blum v. Stenson’s prevailing market rate in the relevant community as opposed to fees determined under contract principles in Johnson v. Georgia Hwy. Exp. Inc., 488 F.2d 714 (5th Cir. 1979) capping fees at lower hourly rates; and b) with a right to recover fees and costs incurred in order to obtain fees.
