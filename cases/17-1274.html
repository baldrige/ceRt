Questions presented

  1. Did the Ninth Circuit err in interpreting Federal Rule of Appellate Procedure 41(d)(1) to permit an appellate court to withhold its mandate indefinitely and thereby retain jurisdiction long after this Court denied a petition for certiorari?

  2. Did the Ninth Circuit err in holding that the Antiterrorism and Effective Death Penalty Act of 1996, 28 U.S.C. § 2254, permits a federal court to disregard a state court’s language and instead presume constitutional error based on the federal court’s perception of state-court error in other cases?
