Questions presented

  1. Whether the Ninth Circuit, in conflict with Jett v. Dallas Independent School Dist., 491 U.S. 701, 724

  2. and nine federal circuits, erroneously permits direct suits under 42 U.S.C. § 1981 against individual state actors instead of requiring prosecution of such claims under 42 U.S.C. § 1983.

  3. Whether municipal employees can be held liable under 42 U.S.C. § 1981 for impairing rights under a contract to which they are not actually parties, in light of Domino’s Pizza, Inc. v. McDonald, 546 U.S. 470, 477 (2006).

  4. Whether the Ninth Circuit improperly invaded the province of state law by allowing public employees to bring § 1981 claims for employment discrimination, when, in California, public employment is conferred by statute, not contract.
