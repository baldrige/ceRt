Question presented

When the district court dismisses all criminal charges against a defendant, does that court have jurisdiction over a motion to expunge the records relating to those charges, as held by the Second, Tenth, and D.C. Circuits, or does the district court lack jurisdiction over such motions, as held by the First, Third, Fourth, Fifth, Sixth, Seventh, and Eleventh Circuits?
