Supreme Court of the United States · No. 19-5807

Thedrick Edwards v. Darrel Vannoy, Warden

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit, No. 18-31095 · judgment May 20, 2019

Decided · May 17, 2021

Question presented

Whether Louisiana's non-unanimous jury requirement for crimes requiring life sentences violates the Sixth Amendment and Fourteenth Amendment of the United States Constitution? LIMITED TO THE FOLLOWING QUESTION: WHETHER THIS COURT’S DECISION IN RAMOS V. LOUISIANA, 590 U. S. ___ (2020), APPLIES RETROACTIVELY TO CASES ON FEDERAL COLLATERAL REVIEW.
