Questions presented

  1. Whether, or under what circumstances, a criminal defendant’s waiver in his plea agreement of the right to appeal his “sentence” covers an appeal of an order of restitution.

  2. Whether, or under what circumstances, a defendant’s waiver of the right to appeal an order of restitution or forfeiture precludes an appeal on the ground that the order exceeds the amount the statutory scheme allows.
