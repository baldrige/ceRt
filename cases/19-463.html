Question presented

Whether a district court may override the order of a state court sealing a court filing necessary to resolve a motion in the state court, by compelling production in federal court of that sealed filing, without requiring that the request for access be made to the state court consistent with principles of comity, federalism, and the State’s ability to perform its judicial functions.
