Supreme Court of the United States · No. 21-5726

Dexter Earl Kemp v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit, No. 20-10958 · judgment May 25, 2021

Decided · June 13, 2022

Question presented

Federal Rule of Civil Procedure 60(b)(1) authorizes relief from final judgment based on “mistake,” as well as inadvertence, surprise, or excusable neglect. The question presented is: Whether Rule 60(b)(1) authorizes relief based on a district court’s error of law.
