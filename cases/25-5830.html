Question presented

Defendants who have at least two prior “controlled substance offense” convictions qualify as career offenders under the federal Sentencing Guidelines. U.S.S.G. § 4B1.2(b). Does the term “controlled substance” broadly include all drugs controlled by federal and state law, or only those substances outlawed by the Controlled Substances Act?
