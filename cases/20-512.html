National Collegiate Athletic Association v. Shawne Alston, et al.

Paid petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, No. 19-15566, 19-15662 · judgment May 18, 2020

Decided · June 21, 2021
Pre-decision estimate: 5% cert probability

Question presented

Consolidated with 20-520. Whether the Ninth Circuit erroneously held, in conflict with decisions of other circuits and general antitrust principles, that the National Collegiate Athletic Association eligibility rules regarding compensation of student-athletes violate federal antitrust law.
