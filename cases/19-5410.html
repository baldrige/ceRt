Supreme Court of the United States · No. 19-5410

Charles Borden, Jr. v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit, No. 18-5409 · judgment April 25, 2019

Decided · June 10, 2021

Questions presented

  1. Does the "use of force" clause in the Armed Career Criminal Act (the "ACCA"), 18 U.S.C. § 924(e)(2)(B)(i) encompass crimes with a mens rea of mere recklessness?

  2. Did the district court violate Mr. Borden's due process rights when it applied to his sentencing a newer, more punitive interpretation of law than that which was in force at the time of his federal offense, such that his guidelines were enhanced from 77 to 96 months to a mandatory minimum sentence of 15 years to life in prison? LIMITED TO QUESTION 1 PRESENTED BY THE PETITION.
