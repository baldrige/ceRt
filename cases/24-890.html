Questions presented

  1. Does the legal standard of evidence require a showing of imminent harm to justify the issuance of an ex parte injunction without notice?

  2. Are employees legally protected from a work place injunction based on a labor dispute without authorization of the workplace and under actions directed by employer?

  3. Is a workplace injunction void for not following labor rights protections required by Title VII of the Civil Rights Act and EEOC including the right to disclosure?
