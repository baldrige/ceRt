Questions presented

  1. Did the Eighth Circuit err in holding that Trooper Black was entitled to qualified immunity on Ms. Hansen’s claim of illegal seizure under the Fourth Amendment for Trooper Black’s fatal shooting of Ms. Hansen’s pet dog because there was no case exactly on point in determining that the law was not clearly established?

  2. Did the Eighth Circuit err in holding that Trooper Black’s fatal shooting of Ms. Hansen’s pet dog was objectively reasonable by applying an excessive force analysis in that a warrantless seizure of personal property is presumed to be unreasonable and is unreasonable when it is more intrusive than necessary?
