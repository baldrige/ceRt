Question presented

I. AND BECAUSE OF THE DOCTOR-PATIENT RELATIONSHIP EXISTING BETWEEN PLAINTIFF AND RESPONDENT, A DUTY EXISTED BECAUSE OF THE RELATIONSHIP ITSELF PURSUANT TO TONEY V.
