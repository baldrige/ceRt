Question presented

Is a state law requiring government contractors to certify that they are not participating in, and will not participate in, boycotts of Israel or Israelcontrolled territories consistent with NAACP v. Claiborne Hardware Co., 458 U.S. 886 (1982) and the First Amendment’s central prohibition against content and viewpoint discrimination?
