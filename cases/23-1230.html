Question presented

Whether an action by the Environmental Protection Agency is “nationally applicable” or “based on a determination of nationwide scope or effect” for purposes of laying venue under 42 U.S.C. §7607(b)(1) when the action uses a common legal requirement and a general factual finding to resolve all pending “small refinery” petitions for exemption from annual obligations under the Renewable Fuel Program irrespective of the petitioning refineries’ location. PARTIES TO THE PROCEEDING Petitioners, intervenors below, are Growth Energy and the Renewable Fuels Association. Respondents, petitioners below, are Calumet Shreveport Refining, L.L.C.; Ergon Refining, Incorporated; Ergon-West Virginia, Inc.; Placid Refining Company, L.L.C.; The San Antonio Refinery, L.L.C; Wynnewood Refining Company, L.L.C. Respondent below was the U.S. Environmental Protection Agency. Other intervenors below were American Coalition for Ethanol, National Corn Growers Association, and National Farmers Union. (i)
