Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-5465

David Nicholson v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Fifth Circuit, No. 23-11198 · judgment January 28, 2025

Certiorari denied · October 6, 2025

Question presented

  1. Whether a conviction under 21 U.S.C. § 841 for possession with intent to distribute a controlled substance can be sustained based solely on (1) constructive possession inferred from joint occupancy or proximity to hidden contraband without clear evidence of dominion and control, and (2) an uncorroborated post-offense admission not made to law enforcement, where no independent evidence confirms the essential element of possession.
