Questions presented

  1. Whether a state’s constitutional obligation to pay just compensation when taking property waives its sovereign immunity from a claim seeking damages for an unconstitutional taking?

  2. Whether a property owner may sue a state official in their personal capacity under 42 U.S.C. § 1983 for a violation of the Takings Clause, as the First Circuit holds, or whether such a personal capacity suit is categorically “barred,” as the Sixth Circuit holds?
