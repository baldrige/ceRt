Question presented

Section 1981 gives every person “the same right . . . to make and enforce contracts . . . as is enjoyed by white citizens. . . .” The question presented is: Does section 1981 prohibit a third party from interfering, on the basis of race, with the right of an individual to make or enforce a contract?
