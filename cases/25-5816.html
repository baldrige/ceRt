William Loydellton Speed, Jr. v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Eighth Circuit, No. 24-1778 · judgment August 18, 2025

Certiorari denied · November 17, 2025

Questions presented

  1. Whether the term "controlled substance" in the definition of "controlled substance offense" in the Career Offender Guideline, § 4B1.2(b), refers to a substance controlled under federal law or under the law of the convicting jurisdiction, resolving the entrenched circuit split on this question?

  2. Whether the circuits are divided on the scope of the buyer-seller exception to drug conspiracy liability under 21 U.S.C. § 846, and whether this case is an excellent vehicle to clarify when a single transaction suffices for conviction?
