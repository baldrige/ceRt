Questions presented

  1. Whether a misrepresentation that does not concern the price or fundamental characteristics of property can give rise to a violation of the federal mail-fraud and wire-fraud statutes, 18 U.S.C. 1341 and 1343.

  2. Whether a defendant may be convicted for making a false statement under 18 U.S.C. 1001 by answering a question posed by a government agent in a way that is ambiguous as to its truth or falsity. (I)
