Pre-decision estimate: 15% cert probability

Before the decision, about 3.3× the 4.4% base rate. The model weighted this up for a Fifth Circuit decision below, a dissent in the court below (flagged in the petition), and a circuit split argued in the petition, and down for a business respondent.

Question presented

Whether the Federal Arbitration Act permits a court to decline to enforce an agreement delegating questions of arbitrability to an arbitrator if the court concludes the claim of arbitrability is "wholly groundless."
