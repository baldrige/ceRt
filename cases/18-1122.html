Questions presented

  1. Did Congress, under 26 U.S.C. §280E, empower the IRS and its civil auditors to investigate federal drug law crimes and administratively determine whether a taxpayer is criminally culpable under federal drug laws?

  2. Is Section 280E – a provision that strips the taxpayer of the benefit of taking otherwise lawful deductions and credits if it is found that the taxpayer is a criminal drug trafficker – a penalty for crime?
