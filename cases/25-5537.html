Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-5537

Benito M. Valdez v. United States

IFP petition · District of Columbia Court of Appeals, No. 18-CF-1340 · judgment August 15, 2024

Certiorari denied · January 12, 2026

Question presented

Is the Sixth Amendment right to a “public trial” violated where the trial court—for the stated purpose of keeping the proceedings private rather than public, but without making any case-specific findings on an overriding need for privacy— conducts an entire phase of the trial at the judge’s bench while using a white-noise machine to prevent anyone in the audience from hearing the trial?
