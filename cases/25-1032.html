Question presented

Whether the Sixteenth Amendment authorizes Congress to tax compensation for labor not arising from the exercise of a federal privilege (i.e., an “excise”)1 without apportionment among the Union States. 1 The terms ‘excise tax’ and ‘privilege tax’ are synonymous. The two are often used interchangeably. American Airways v. Wallace, 57 F.2d 877, 880 (Dist. Ct., MD. Tenn., 1932).
