Questions presented

  1. “Invited Error” Violation. Did the Fifth Circuit err by reversing because of jury instructions that the Government itself drafted?

  2. United Dominion Violation. Did the Fifth Circuit err by interpreting an undefined and unlimited term (“Impediment”) in favor of the IRS and against the taxpayer?

  3. Neder Violation. Did the Fifth Circuit err by reversing because of jury instructions when overwhelming evidence would have supported the same result with different jury instructions?

  4. Rule 51 Violations. Did the Fifth Circuit err by reversing because of a: (1) jury instruction that the Government never objected to at trial; and/or (2) verdict form that the Government never objected to at trial?

  5. Attorney Fee Award. If the Fifth Circuit erred for any of the reasons stated above, did it then also err by vacating the attorney fee award for RSBCO?
