IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit, No. 21-10233 · judgment January 4, 2022

Certiorari denied · October 14, 2025

Question presented

Whether a 2254 application is second or successive if it challenges a new judgment for purpose of §2244(b). Whether a new judgment, resulting from resentencing, triggers or restarts the statute of limitations for purposes of §2244(d)(1)(A). Whether a clearly erroneous decision of the court of appeals denies procedural due process (liberty interest) under the Fifth or Fourteenth Amendment.
