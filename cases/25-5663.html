Question presented

Whether a United States District Court judge may dismiss a civil rights employment discrimination case on procedural technicalities without granting: A jury trial as requested under Rule 38, A hearing on a motion to proceed in forma pauperis (IFP) despite documented indigency and medical hardship, Appointment of counsel, and A full hearing on religious accommodation, constitutional violations (First and Fourteenth Amendments), and EEOC findings — all in contravention of Caperton v. Massey, Haines v. Kerner, and Henderson v. United States?
