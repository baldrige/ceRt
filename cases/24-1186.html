Justice Gorsuch has stated that “[D]ecisive executive action is sometimes necessary and appropriate. But if emergency decrees promise to solve some problems, they threaten to generate others.” Arizona v. Mayorkas, No 22-592, 598 U.S. — (May 18, 2023).
