Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-6089

Curtis Solomon v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Eleventh Circuit, No. 22-11488 · judgment May 15, 2025

Certiorari denied · January 12, 2026

Question presented

Whether the Eleventh Circuit’s recent iteration of its “prior panel precedent rule”—which requires rigid adherence to prior circuit precedent, even if the prior precedent indisputably did not apply a new “mode of analysis” dictated by an intervening decision of this Court—contravenes well settled principles of vertical stare decisis, and denies Eleventh Circuit defendants their statutory right to appeal and due process of law, in conflict with the approach of other circuits.
