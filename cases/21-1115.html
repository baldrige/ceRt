Questions presented

Petitioner appealed to the Fifth Circuit Appellate Court of Appeals over abuse of discretion of three(3) distinct issues tightly relating to each other. The Fifth Circuit issued an opinion which omitted addressing the first and third issues. Petitioner motioned for the court to address those issues because if there were findings of abuse of discretion, the second issue automatically would be an abuse of discretion, contradictory to their opinion.
