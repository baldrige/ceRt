Questions presented

  1. Whether state Medicaid payments to hospitals that provide services to disproportionately lowincome patients constitute a refund of costs those hospitals incurred in paying state taxes ordinarily reimbursable by Medicare, such that those taxes are no longer “actually incurred” costs for purposes of Medicare reimbursement.

  2. Whether courts should defer to agency interpretation of statutes or regulations that involved embedded questions of state law.
