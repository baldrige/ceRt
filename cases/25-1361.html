Questions presented

In addition to the reversal of the November 24, 2025, Judgment of the U.S. Court of Appeals for the Sixth Circuit, the questions for which Petitioners seek certiorari are as follows:

  1. Whether this Court’s Opinion in Knick, supra, permits a federal cause of action against a municipal entity for an uncompensated physical taking when the municipal entity has materially impaired the value and precluded the alienability of the Petitioners’ property.

  2. Whether the actions of the Village in singling out the Petitioners’ property and engaging in concerted actions to prevent its sale at auction are sufficient to state a claim for violation of Petitioners’ rights to due process and equal protection under the Fourteenth Amendment to the Constitution.
