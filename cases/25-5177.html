Questions presented

  1. Does a state judge’s refusal to follow federal law and a prosecutor’s claim under oath that “the Republic is dead” violate due process and the Supremacy Clause?

  3. Do exorbitant transcript costs and suppressed body camera footage deny access to justice and a fair trial?

  4. Does Missouri’s address paradox disproportionately criminalize transients, violating equal protection?
