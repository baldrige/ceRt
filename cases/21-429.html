Questions presented

  1. Whether a State has authority to prosecute non-Indians who commit crimes against Indians in Indian country.

  2. Whether McGirt v. Oklahoma, 140 S. Ct. 2452 (2020), should be overruled. GRANTED LIMITED TO QUESTION 1 PRESENTED BY THE PETITION. THE CASE WILL BE SET FOR ARGUMENT IN THE APRIL 2022 ARGUMENT SESSION.
