West Virginia, et al. v. Environmental Protection Agency, et al.

Paid petition · United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit, No. 19-1140, 19-1165, 19-1166, 19-1173, 19-1175, 19-1176, 19-1177, 19-1179, 19-1185, 19-1186, 19-1187, 19-1188 · judgment January 19, 2021

Pre-decision estimate: 2% cert probability

Question presented

In 42 U.S.C. § 7411(d), an ancillary provision of the Clean Air Act, did Congress constitutionally authorize the Environmental Protection Agency to issue significant rulesincluding those capable of reshaping the nation's electricity grids and unilaterally decarbonizing virtually any sector of the economy-without any limits on what the agency can require so long as it considers cost, nonair impacts, and energy requirements? Consolidated with 20-1531, 20-1778, and 20-1780 and a total of one hour is allotted for oral argument
