Question presented

Did the trial court commit reversible error’s dismissing the petition for an injunction to prevent Respondent from stalking? Did the court violate the procedural due process clause, enshrined in the Fifth and Fourteenth Amendments of the United States Constitution when it denied Petitioner relief without affording him a hearing in violation of the Petitioner’s due process right to notice, opportunity to be heard and the right to have a decision from an impartial decision-maker as guaranteed by the 5th and 14th amendments to the United States Constitution? Did the officer violate the Petitioners 4th Amendment rights? Are the trial courts in violation of 18 U.S.C. § Section 242 Deprivation of Rights under Color of Law? Did the judge commit an error by not disqualifying themselves?
