Question presented

The question presented is whether the Younger abstention doctrine applies when a state has indicated it will disregard the Supremacy Clause of the Constitution and this Court's dicta in Sperry, when it attempts to regulate a non-state party who has no significant ties to a state, other than representing a resident of that state before a federal agency based in D.C., given the clear federal objectives that Congress wants no state lawyer's bar regulation over those representing federal employees before a federal agency.
