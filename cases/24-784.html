Question presented

Did the Sixth Circuit err in not applying the rule from SEC v. Chenery Corp., 318 U.S. 80, 93 (1943), which requires courts to evaluate agency action on “the grounds upon which the agency acted,” to cases arising under the Black Lung Benefits Act, in direct conflict with the Fourth and Seventh Circuits?
