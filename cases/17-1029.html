Questions presented

App. Dist. 12014, no pet.). ) is applicable to RICO 4.Whether attorney litigant, guardian ad litem and attorney ad litem immunity under Texas law is applicable to RICO:

  5. Whether a trial Court may award and the Fifth Circuit may affirm pro se attorney attorney’s fees, as sanctions,
