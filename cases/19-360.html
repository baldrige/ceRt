QUESTIONS FOE REVIEW

Shall this Court allow any judge to abuse his or her power and illegally violate the Constitution of the United States and unconstitutionally deprive us of our civil rights and other rights guaranteed to us by the Constitution of the United States, including, but not limited to, our right to he heard?
