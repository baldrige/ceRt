Question presented

Whether the decisions below violated the Federal Arbitration Act and decades of Court precedent by failing to enforce the express terms of the parties’ Arbitration Agreements; and Whether the decisions below violated the Federal Arbitration Act by delegating the decision as to whether to award attorneys’ fees and costs to the trial court on remand, as opposed to vacating the arbitrator’s award and leaving open the possibility of further proceedings under the Arbitration Agreements.
