Question presented

Whether the Commerce Clause requires states to consider a taxpayer’s burden in light of the state tax scheme as a whole when crediting a taxpayer’s out-ofstate tax liability as the West Virginia and Colorado Supreme Courts have held and this Court has suggested, or permits states to credit out-of-state state and local tax liabilities as discrete tax burdens, as the Pennsylvania Supreme Court held below.
