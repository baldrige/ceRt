Question presented

Whether information obtained in violation of the Fifth Amendment’s SelfIncrimination Clause can establish the probable cause necessary to authorize a Tennessee police officer’s warrantless seizure of a firearm from a vehicle under the Fourth Amendment’s plain view exception when the illegal nature of the firearm is not “immediately apparent” absent the Miranda violation.
