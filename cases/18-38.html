Questions presented

Whether, in defense of a whistleblower claim of wrongful termination by a probationary employee, pursuant to 5 U.S.C. § 1221, the agency may claim that it would have taken the same personnel action in the absence of such disclosure based upon grounds which were specifically rejected by the agency in the first instance?
