Question presented

The question presented is: Whether punitively depriving a prisoner in solitary confinement of virtually all exercise for three years notwithstanding the absence of a security justification violates the Eighth Amendment, as ten circuits hold, or whether such a denial only violates the Eighth Amendment if it is imposed in response to an “utterly trivial infraction,” as the court below, but no other circuit, holds.
