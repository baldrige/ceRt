Questions presented

  1. Whether the Fourteenth Amendment’s Due Process Clause is violated when a timely notice of appeal is denied solely because of the State’s malfunctioning mandatory electronic filing system.

  2. Whether Equal Protection is violated when litigants are treated differently based solely on random technological failures in State systems.

  3. Whether the fundamental right of access to courts prohibits a State from extinguishing appellate review due to government-created technical barriers.

  4. Whether adverse judicial treatment of a litigant's constitutional objections constitutes retaliation in violation of the First Amendment’s Petition Clause. II.
