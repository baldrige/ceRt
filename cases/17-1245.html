Question presented

This case presents a question nearly identical to the second question presented in Animal Science Products, Inc., v. Hebei Welcome Pharmaceutical Co., Ltd., No. 16-1220, on which this Court granted review. The question presented is: Whether a foreign sovereign’s interpretation of its domestic law is entitled to conclusive deference (as held by the Second Circuit in Animal Science Products), significant deference, as recognized by the Seventh Circuit and as advocated now by the U.S. Solicitor General, or no deference, as implied by the Ninth Circuit in this action?
