Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-6132

Wesley Mark Sudbury v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit, No. 22-10265 · judgment June 6, 2025

Certiorari denied · January 12, 2026

Questions presented

  1. Whether a mandatory statute enacted by Congress to protect an important fundamental right titled Prohibition of use as evidence of intercepted wire or oral communications, coupled with another mandatory statute titled Litigation concerning sources of evidence, are being uniformly applied as Congress intended.

  2. When 18 USC 3504 is invoked, what is the burden on the defendant, and what is the burden on the Government.

  3. Have additional burdens to section 3504, not intended by Congress, been given to defendants.
