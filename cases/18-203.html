Questions presented

  1. Whether the Eighth Amendment requires the sentencing authority to make a finding that a juvenile is permanently incorrigible before imposing a sentence of life without parole.

  2. Whether Joey Chandler’s life without parole sentence violates the Eighth Amendment because the sentencing judge failed to consider substantial, unrebutted evidence of his rehabilitation.
