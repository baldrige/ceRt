Questions presented

This petition presents three questions pertaining to an employment discrimination plaintiffs evidentiary burden in summary judgment proceedings to prove the existence of a genuine issue for trial:

  1. Whether a plaintiff must demonstrate that comparators proffered to show disparate treatment are “nearly identical,” as the D.C. Circuit requires, or only “similarly situated,” as most other circuits require;

  2. Whether a court may dismiss as mere “opinion” a plaintiff s sworn factual rebuttals to disciplinary charges rather than accepting them as evidence of disputed facts; and

  3. Whether a court must consider as material evidence of pretext facts proving that a governmental employer violated due process in removing an employee.
