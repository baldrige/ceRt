Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-5959

Jonathan Voorhis v. Cindy Digangi, et al.

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit, No. 24-2725 · judgment April 24, 2025

Certiorari denied · December 8, 2025

Question presented

Questions:. Is a child protective services (CPS) worker entitled to absolute immunity under 42 U.S.C. § 1983 for allegedly fabricating evidence when the record does not clearly establish that the conduct was judicial in nature? Question left unanswered: Can a court grant summary judgment before discovery based on a purported absence of evidence, when the plaintiffs complaint identifies material evidence already in the defendant’s possession, and the defendant neither disputes nor acknowledges that evidence?
