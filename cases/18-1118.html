Question presented

When the standing of a removing defendant is challenged in the Court of Appeals must that Court directly address such standing challenge pursuant to its independent and sua sponte duty to establish that the presumption against lower federal courts subject matter jurisdiction has been rebutted? Is the presumption against subject matter jurisdiction of federal courts rebutted by Washington procedural law?
