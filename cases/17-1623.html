Question presented

Consolidated with 17-1618. Whether the prohibition in Title VII of the Civil Rights Act of 1964, 42 U.S.C. § 2000e-2(a) (l), against employment discrimination "because of . . . sex" encompasses discrimination based on an individual's sexual orientation.
