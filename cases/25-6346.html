Questions presented

  1. Whether trial counsel was ineffective and the trial court s unconstitutional jury instruction derived from Pennsylvania's consolidated statute 18 Pa.C.S.A. § 6104 violated the 6th and 14th Amendments?

Biggers, 409 U.S. 188(1972)?
