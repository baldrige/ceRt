Pre-decision estimate: 5% cert probability

Before the decision, roughly the 4.4% base rate. The model weighted this up for a D.C. Circuit decision below and a dissent in the court below (flagged in the petition), and down for a business respondent.

Question presented

Consolidated with 19-760. The Committee on Oversight and Reform of the U.S. House of Representatives has issued a subpoena to the accountant for President Trump and several of his business entities. The subpoena demands private financial records belonging to the President. The D.C. Circuit upheld the subpoena as having a legitimate legislative purpose and being within the statutory authority of the Committee. The question presented is: Whether the Committee has the constitutional and statutory authority to issue this subpoena.
