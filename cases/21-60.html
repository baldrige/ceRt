Question presented

In a case where the prosecution charged both a greater offense and a lesser included offense in the same charging document, and where the jury found the Petitioner guilty of the greater offense – but not guilty of the lesser included offense – whether constitutional double jeopardy principles prohibit a conviction on the greater offense in light of the jury’s not guilty verdict for the lesser included offense.
