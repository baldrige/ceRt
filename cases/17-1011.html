Supreme Court of the United States · No. 17-1011

Budha Ismail Jam, et al. v. International Finance Corporation

Paid petition · United States Court of Appeals for the District of Columbia Circuit, No. 16-7051 · judgment June 23, 2017

Decided · February 27, 2019
Pre-decision estimate: 2% cert probability

Questions presented

  1. Whether the International Organizations Immunities Act-which affords international organizations the "same immunity" from suit that foreign governments have, 22 U.S.C. § 288a (b)- confers the same immunity on such organizations as foreign governments have under the Foreign Sovereign Immunities Act, 28 U.S.C. §§ 1602-11.

  2. If not, what are the rules governing the immunity to which international organizations are entitled? GRANTED LIMITED TO QUESTION 1 PRESENTED BY THE PETITION. JUSTICE KAVANAUGH IS RECUSED IN THIS CASE.
