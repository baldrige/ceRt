Question presented

Whether the Circuit Court violated Petitioner's constitutional right to fully present his case and his constitutional right to raise his daughter in a manner free of arbitrary judicial intervention by: Refusing to re-open the evidence to allow Petitioner to submit evidence that directly pertained to his competency as a parent; Refusing to conduct an in camera interview with the minor-daughter to determine whether she was competent to testify, despite the fact that she was the sole witness of an assault Respondent committed against Petitioner; Arbitrarily barring the daughter from playing competitive golf for a year despite the absence of any evidence suggesting that the golfing was having any adverse consequences on the child.
