Question presented

When petitioners claim that sale order of bankruptcy court is void, claimed ownership of the property in social media, urged full disclosure of the fact and filed lawsuit, are petitioners barred to exercise First Amendment right because of the sale order? Whether Chapter 7 trustee violated multiple constitutional rights of petitioners with the violation of discharge injunction, improperly awarded his fees because of the sale order? Whether the sale order exceeds the jurisdiction of bankruptcy court to bar petitioners' future lawsuit to recover the house? Then, where is the boundary of its jurisdiction?
