Questions presented

  1. Whether something with no commercial or objective value can constitute a “thing of value” that “exceed[s] $1,000,” the receipt of which is punishable as a felony under the bank bribery statute, 18 U.S.C. § 215.

  2. Whether the bank bribery statute’s “corrupt” intent element requires a breach of a bank officer’s duty to act in the best interests of the bank or can instead be satisfied by acts undertaken with a good-faith belief they will benefit the bank.
