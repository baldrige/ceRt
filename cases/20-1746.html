Question presented

Petitioner was born in Afghanistan. He immigrated to the United States and became a United States citizen. His siblings and other relatives remained in Afghanistan and petitioner had real property in Afghanistan. Whether a United States citizen can be denied his Fifth Amendment rights to just compensation for property taken by the United States based upon foreign law.
