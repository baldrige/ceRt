Question presented

Under 35 U.S.C. § 271(a), a person directly infringes a patent whenever she “offers to sell” a patented invention “within the United States.” The question presented is whether an “offer[] to sell” occurs where the offer is actually made or where the offer contemplates that the proposed sale will take place.
