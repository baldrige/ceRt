Questions presented

  1. Are forgery and robo-signing one and the same?

  2. Are there due process and equal protection issues surrounding the determination as to whether the bad act is robo-signing or forgery?

  3. Is it a violation of due process and equal protection of the law to forbid a victim of forgery to use the judicial process in order to cure his loss?

  4. Is it a violation of due process and equal protection of the law to forbid a homeowner the right to challenge defects in the chain of title to his property that led to the loss of his property?
