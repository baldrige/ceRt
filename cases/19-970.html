Questions presented

  1. Does the United States have complete immunity from constitutional counterclaims asserted in an in rem civil forfeiture case initiated by the United States?

  2. Does the seizure of funds from a bank account without notice and a hearing violate the Due Process Clause of the Fifth Amendment when Congress has provided for injunctions and restraining orders as an alternative?
