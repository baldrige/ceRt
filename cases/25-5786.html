Vamsidhar Vurimindi v. Pennsylvania

IFP petition · Superior Court of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia Office, No. 2272 EDA 2023 · judgment February 5, 2025

Dismissed · November 10, 2025

Question presented

I. Whether a state violates the Due Process Clause by abolishing the writ of error coram nobis and then rigidly applying a post-conviction custody requirement to permanently bar any judicial review of a conviction that is void ab initio under the Double Jeopardy Clause of the Fifth Amendment? II. Whether the Double Jeopardy Clause permits a state to re-litigate an issue of ultimate fact, a defendant’s criminal intent, that was necessarily determined by a prior acquittal, merely by alleging new acts that are used to bootstrap the acquitted conduct as pattern evidence?
