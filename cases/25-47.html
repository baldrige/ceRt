Question presented

A. Did the courts below properly apply SCDOT zero tolerance harassment policies. B. If a company prepares a salary study with errors and only compares one black employee salary to one white employee salary that have the same job and similar experience, is the internally produced salary study with errors unreliable to satisfy Title VII evidence burden of production. AICPA and GAAP independence rules or would an accurate externally prepared salary analysis based on third party data comparing multiple employees be more reliable? C.
