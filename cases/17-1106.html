Question presented

Whether the court of appeals violated the deferential review requirements of 28 U.S.C. § 2254(d) by setting aside a state conviction based on its de novo analysis of an ineffective-assistance claim, without fulfilling its obligation to consider whether fairminded jurists could agree with the state court’s contrary conclusion.
