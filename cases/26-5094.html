Questions presented

  1. Whether the sentencing of the Petitioner under 21 U.S.C. § 841 (a)(1), and (b)(1)(B), for possession with intent to distribute a controlled substance violate the petitioner's rights under the First Step Act, given the Petitioner's minimum-role in the offense.

  2. Whether the sentence imposed by the Tenth Circuit conflicts with decisions of other United States Courts of Appeals regarding sentence reduction for minimal-role participants under 21 U.S.C. § 841 (a)(1), and (b)(1)(B).
