Questions presented

  1. Whether a law enforcement officer acting as a private citizen who initiates a traffic stop with no intention of ever attempting to complete the “mission” of the traffic stop has the authority to prolong the duration of the traffic stop in five discretely different ways while waiting on a drug dog to arrive?

  2. Whether a law enforcement officer acting as a private citizen may permissibly conduct a “citizen’s arrest” for the offense of speeding, even though the statute governing the offense of speeding requires the issuance of a traffic citation to the person in lieu of arrest, continued custody, and the taking of the arrested person before a magistrate?
