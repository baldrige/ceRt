Question presented

Whether a party injured by antitrust behavior leading to a monopolistic end has standing for damage relief under the Sherman Act’s 15 U.S.C. §§ 1, 2, and 15. Whether the court or a jury determines when conspirators’ violation of the antitrust act is sufficiently “impending” to inflict injury allowing for damages under the Sherman Act’s 15 U.S.C. §§ 1, 2, and 15.
