Questions presented

  1. Does a municipality’s refusal to renew the operating license of an established business, thereby causing the destruction of the business, constitute a deprivation of property under the Due Process Clause of the Fourteenth Amendment?

  2. If a municipality violates an established business’s procedural due process rights, which party bears the burden of proving whether the due process violation caused injury?
