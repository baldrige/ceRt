Question presented

Does a school district have an affirmative obligation to provide pendency-related educational and support services to its special education students under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act ("IDEA"), 20 U.S.C. §1400, et seq.? If a school district has such an obligation, may the parent of a special education student procure the pendency services to which the student is entitled under the IDEA, when the district has failed to procure them?
