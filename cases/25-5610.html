Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-5610

Greg Gogin v. Michael Meisner, Warden

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit, No. 24-1957 · judgment January 22, 2025

Certiorari denied · November 10, 2025

Question presented

Whether a defendant's sworn testimony while on the witness stand and under oath should be given credence as to the facts of his case and can defendant be t given a "second chance" to correct a wrong. Whether a defendant has a right to correct any statements made to a Presentence Investigator while under duress. Whether vague, unsure, and incomplete statements from a victim can be taken as facts and then expounded on by the court. Whether a defendant's rights to be sentenced on accurate information have been violated contradictory to 28 U.S.C.A. § 2254(d)(l)(2).
