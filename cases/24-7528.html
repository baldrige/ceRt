John A. Sam v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the First Circuit, No. 24-1090 · judgment April 22, 2025

Certiorari denied · October 6, 2025

Question presented

In February 2017, Petitioner John Sam was convicted of robbery in violation of Maine law, a felony offense. In May 2021, the police found Sam in possession of a firearm and five rounds of ammunition. In April 2023, Sam pleaded guilty to violating 18 U.S.C. § 922(g)(1), which codifies a lifetime ban on firearm possession by convicted felons. The question presented is: Does the application of Section 922(g)(1) to Sam plainly violate the Second Amendment?
