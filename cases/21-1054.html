See Gabelli v. Sec. & Exch. Common, 568 U.S. 442 (2013). Rule 15(c)(1) of the Federal Rule of Civil Procedures (Fed. R. Civ. Proc.) permits “[a]n amend­ ment to a pleading relates back to the date of the orig­ inal pleading when (A) the law that provides the applicable statute of limitations allows relation back; (B) the amendment asserts a claim or defense that arose out of the conduct... in the original pleading”. A fraud, if concealed and can only be unearthed by discovery, may render its victim’s pleading prior to the discovery implausible due to the unavailability of crit­ ical evidence.
