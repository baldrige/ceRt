Questions presented

Petitioners were appointed to the Boards of Visitors of the three United States military academies. These Boards are advisory groups subject to the Federal Advisory Committees Act, and are not Executive Branch agencies. To ensure that the Boards exercise their “independent judgment,” are not “inappropriately inf luenced by the appointing authority,” the terms of presidentially appointed Board members are statutorily set at three years. The statutes do not authorize the President to remove Board members.
