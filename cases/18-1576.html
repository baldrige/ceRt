Questions presented

The 86th legislature in Texas has changed the law and having provided an guidance to the Texas Courts about the applicability of the Texas Citizens’ Participation Act (TCPA) for the tests what counts as a definition for matter of public concern from the jurisprudence of the U. S. Supreme Court case, Snyder v. Phelps, 562 U.S. 443 (2011).

  1. Does the TCPA apply to a private employment dispute that involved allegedly defamatory statements in a private intended audience and being in a private location?

  2. The TCPA (Texas Citizens' Participation Act) has been changed and will take effective on 09/01/2019. Whether the change in law of the TCPA would have benefited the petitioner about the pending case or not?
