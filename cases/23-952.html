Questions presented

  1. Whether claims seeking damages for the effects of interstate and international emissions on the global climate are beyond the limits of state law and thus preempted under the federal Constitution.

  2. Whether the Clean Air Act preempts state-law claims predicated on damaging interstate emissions.
