Jesus Herrera-Salazar v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Tenth Circuit, No. 24-7029 · judgment September 26, 2025

Pending

Question presented

Whether the Double Jeopardy Clause prohibits a second prosecution for the crime of illegally reentry, specifically, the “found in” offense under 8 U.S.C. § 1326(a), where the government fails to show that, after the first prosecution of the defendant for being found in the United States, the defendant was removed from the country and later reentered the country illegally.
