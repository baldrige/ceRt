Question presented

Whether the court below erred in holding—in conflict with the decisions of multiple other courts—that an inapplicable assumption of risk instruction does not constitute reversible error in a FELA case, even though giving the instruction in such a case undermines the railroad’s contributory negligence defense and frustrates the FELA’s fault apportionment scheme repeatedly recognized and enforced by this Court.
