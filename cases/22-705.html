Questions presented

The Applicants put forth the following Questions Presented for this Court’s review, to wit:

  1. Does 50 U.S.C.A. §3936 apply to toll a state statute of limitations on a state law foreclosure action where (a) a servicemember voluntarily filed a bankruptcy petition, (b) voluntarily turned his property over to the trustee for administration, ( c) the property was abandoned by the trustee for the express purpose of foreclosure, after a contradictory hearing, and (d) the servicemember did not seek any protections of the Servicemembers Civil Relief Act in the bankruptcy court?

  2. Does 50 U.S.C.A. §3936 apply to toll a state statute of limitations on a pre-petition debt where the servicemember has been discharged in a Chapter 7 bankruptcy proceeding?
