Question presented

In order to obtain a permit to carry a handgun in the public, the State of New Jersey requires a showing of “justifiable need” which, as defined, does not include general self-defense, and thus precludes the ordinary citizen from obtaining such a permit. The question presented is whether the legislative requirement of “justifiable need” for a permit to carry a handgun in public violates the Second Amendment.
