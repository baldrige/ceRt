Question presented

In Hartman v. Moore, 54 7 U.S. 250 (2006), this Court held that probable cause defeats a First Amendment retaliatory-prosecution claim under 42 U.S.C. § 1983 as a matter oflaw. Does probable cause likewise defeat a First Amendment retaliatory-arrest claim under § 1983?
