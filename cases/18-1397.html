Question presented

Whether the Federal Circuit Court of Appeals may retroactively expand the scope of its appellate jurisdiction to invalidate patent claims under 35 U.S.C. § 101 when those claims were not raised in the petitioner’s appeal or necessary for its judgment? Whether a district court may disregard a mandate from the Federal Circuit for entry of judgment, and ignore this Court’s precedent, by retroactively applying the collateral estoppel doctrine based on a ruling in a subsequent action where there is no mutuality of claims or defenses?
