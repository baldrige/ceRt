Question presented

Does Federal Rule of Evidence 704(a) (“Rule 704(a)”) allow an expert to opine that a person’s conduct complied with the person’s legal duties? Does Rule 704(a) allow an expert to opine on the nature and extent of a person’s legal duties? Did the Ninth Circuit err in holding that under Rule 704(a), Respondents’ expert witness was allowed to testify that Respondents’ conduct complied with their legal duties to Kleidman? Did the Ninth Circuit err in holding that under Rule 704(a), Respondents’ expert witness was allowed to testify on the nature and extent of Respondents’ legal duties to Kleidman?
