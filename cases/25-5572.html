Question presented

WHETHER THE PETITIONER'S RIGHT OT DUE PROCESS UNDER THE FOURTEENTH AMENDMENT WAS VIOLATED WHEN THE CLERK OF COURT FOR THE FOUTH CIRCUIT COURT OF APPEALS CAUSED PETITIONER'S APPEAL TO BE DISMISSED AS A RESULT OF MALFEASANCE WHEN THE CLERK SHELVED PETITIONER'S APPEAL FOR OVER A YEAR BY FAILING TO PLACE SAID APPEAL ON THE CALENDAR FOR PANEL REVIEW PURSUANT TO 4TH CIRCUIT COURT OF APPEALS LOCAL RULE R.A.P. WHETHER THE PETITIONER'S RIGHT OT DUE PROCESS UNDER THE FOURTEENTH AMENDMENT WAS VIOLATED WHEN THE FOUTH CIRCUIT COURT OF APPEALS SUMMARILY DISMISSED PETITIONER'S APPEAL AS A RESULT OF A PERFUNCTORY AND ROTE REVIEW OF ALL CLAIMS RAISED IN THE APPEAL, IF SAID APPEAL WAS REVIEWED AT ALL.
