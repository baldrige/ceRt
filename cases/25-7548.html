Questions presented

  1. Whether Rule 24(a)(2) requires intervention as of right when the proposed intervenors constitutional claim cannot be protected in the no-record municipal forum where he was prosecuted.

  3. Whether a statewide right-to-counsel remedy may be rendered ineffective when the municipal court proceeds without the constitutional safeguards required by the Betschart protective order.
