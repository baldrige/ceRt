Question presented

Whether a state tax scheme that taxes the intangible income of individuals who are domiciled in the State and certain individuals not domiciled in the State, without offsetting credits for taxes paid to another State of domicile, violates the dormant Commerce Clause under this Court’s decision in Comptroller of Treasury of Maryland v. Wynne, 135 S. Ct. 1787 (2015). (I)
