Supreme Court of the United States · No. 24-7487

Nicholas Noelani D. Smith v. Alabama

IFP petition · Court of Criminal Appeals of Alabama, No. CR-2022-0504 · judgment June 28, 2024

Certiorari denied · October 6, 2025

Question presented

Whether a criminal defendant in a capital trial is deprived of his Fourteenth Amendment right to due process, as set forth in Beck v. Alabama, when the evidence at trial would support a finding of voluntary intoxication negating specific intent as an element of capital murder, but the trial court fails to instruct the jury to consider any lesser-included offenses to capital murder.
