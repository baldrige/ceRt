Question presented

Whether Austin’s $350 base limit on campaign contributions violates the First Amendment; and Whether Zimmerman established standing to challenge Austin’s aggregate limit on the total amount of campaign contributions a candidate may accept “from sources other than natural persons eligible to vote in a postal zip code completely or partially within the Austin city limits.”
