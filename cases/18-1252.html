Question presented

Is a Search Warrant (for DNA) "reasonable" under the Fourth (4th) Amendment? Was the Petitioner's Sixth (6th) Amendment, "Right to Counsel," violated when Petitioner was denied counsel while he was detained, served with a Search Warrant (for DNA), and had his DNA seized? Is the Fourteenth (14th) Amendment, "Due Process" clause, violated under 42 U.S.C. §14132(d), "Expungement of Records", a statute which provides for an Arrestee to expunge his DNA records, • but does not provide for a Non-Arrestee to expunge his DNA record?
