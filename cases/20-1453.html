Question presented

Does the Federal Aviation Administration Authorization Act, which expressly preempts state laws “related to a price, route, or service of any motor carrier,” 49 U.S.C. § 14501(c)(1), preempt state worker-classification laws that have an effect on a motor carrier’s prices and services by discouraging the use of independent contractors?
