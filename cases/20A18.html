  2. Jul 30 2020
    Application (20A18) granted by the Court. The application for stay presented to JUSTICE KAGAN and by her referred to the Court is granted. The district court’s June 23, June 26, and June 30, 2020 orders are stayed pending disposition of the appeal in the United States Court of Appeals for the Ninth Circuit and disposition of the petition for a writ of certiorari, if such writ is timely sought. Should the petition for a writ of certiorari be denied, this stay shall terminate automatically. In the event the petition for a writ of certiorari is granted, the stay shall terminate upon the sending down of the judgment of this Court. CHIEF JUSTICE ROBERTS, with whom JUSTICE ALITO, JUSTICE GORSUCH, and JUSTICE KAVANAUGH join, concurring in the grant of stay. (Detached Opinion). JUSTICE SOTOMAYOR, with whom JUSTICE GINSBURG joins, dissenting from the grant of stay. (Detached Opinion).
