Dazmine Erving v. United States

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Seventh Circuit, No. 23-2828, 23-2831 · judgment January 20, 2026

Certiorari denied · June 22, 2026

Questions presented

  1. Does the clarification of the “immediate control” standard in Arizona v. Gant, 556 U.S. 332, 341 (2009), apply to protective searches of automobiles under Michigan v. Long, 463 U.S. 1032, 1049 (1983)?

  2. May the government rely on the protective-sweep doctrine by merely arguing that the officer could have conducted a protective search, even if the record shows that the officer objectively did not conduct a protective search and the government failed to develop any inevitable-discovery argument?
