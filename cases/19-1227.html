Question presented

With respect to the surviving spouse class members whose retiree-spouses died on or after January 10, 2005 (after expiration of the 1998 contracts) (“Group 2”), did the Sixth Circuit misapply M&G Polymers USA, LLC v. Tackett, 135 S.Ct. 926 (2015), and related cases and misconstrue the applicable collectivelybargained agreements to find Group 2 surviving spouses have no right to healthcare benefits after the expiration of the agreements?
