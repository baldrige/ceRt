Question presented

Petitioner filed a multi-count complaint. Respondents answered with multiple defenses and moved for summary judgment. The trial court granted summary judgment without opinion. The state supreme court affirmed without opinion. Petitioner’s application for rehearing was denied without opinion. The question presented is whether due process under §1 of the Fourteenth Amendment to the U.S. Constitution entitles the plaintiff to a judgment that explains why her case was dismissed and allows her, in at least one point in the judicial proceeding, a fair opportunity to challenge with specificity that the trial court erred or that the appellate court overlooked or misapprehended a point of law or fact.
