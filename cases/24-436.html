Question presented

Whether, once counsel has been appointed for an indigent defendant, the Sixth Amendment guarantees the defendant the same right to continued representation by that counsel as is enjoyed by defendants affluent enough to retain counsel. The same question is presented in Davis v. Colorado, No. 23-1096, pet. for cert. filed Apr. 5, 2024.
