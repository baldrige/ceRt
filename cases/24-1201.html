Question presented

  1. Whether a public school district acts with deliberate indifference to its students being assaulted inside its school buses after it installed video cameras in all district buses in response to a prior incident of a student being sexually assaulted by a bus driver, but intentionally enacts a policy in which (i) the video cameras at its campuses and facilities are continually live monitored, but (ii) the bus cameras are not monitored and the footage is only reviewed unless and until a report of misconduct is made, thereby causing the sexual assault of M.R.A. by a different bus driver, who testified that he would not have committed the assaults had he known someone was watching the cameras.
