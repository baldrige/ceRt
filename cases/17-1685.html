Questions presented

  1. Whether the Code which requires property owners to consent to an inspection through either a town official or a third party who is required to report to the Town any violations of federal, state, county or town statutes, rules or regulations where there is no cause for a warrant is permitted by the Fourth Amendment.

  2. Whether a property owner has a right to privacy when filing an application for a rental permit.

  3. Whether the Code which requires compliance with current federal, state, county and town codes, rules, regulations and statutes is overbroad and results in a forfeiture of existing Certificates of Occupancy.
