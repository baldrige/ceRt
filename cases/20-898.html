Question presented

  1. Whether the Civil Rights Act of 1866, 42 U.S.C. § 1981 (“Section 1981”) prohibits a non-contracting party from interfering with person’s contract, speciﬁcally, a person’s employment, based on race.

  2. Whether alleging that a party either participated in race discrimination directly or had knowledge of discrimination, the authority to prevent discrimination, and refused to prevent the discrimination, is sufﬁcient to state a cause of action under Section 1981.
