Questions presented

  1. Can arguments in support of a court’s subject-matter jurisdiction be waived?

Does § 636(b)(1) permit a district court to find that a party has waived de novo review of an objection to a magistrate judge’s report and recommendations because the issue or argument was not first raised before the magistrate?
