Question presented

Whether individual Medicaid recipients have a private right of action under 42 U.S.C. § 1396a(a)(23) to challenge the merits of a state’s disqualification of a Medicaid provider. (The question presented is essentially the same presented by the petition in Andersen v. Planned Parenthood of Kansas and MidMissouri, Case No. 17-1340.)
