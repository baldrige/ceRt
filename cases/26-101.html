Question presented

Whether a criminal defendant’s right to be present at all critical stages of trial includes the right to be present for a trial court’s response to a jury’s request for additional instructions. Whether a defendant must show that his presence during the trial court’s response to a jury’s request for additional instructions would have changed the outcome of trial. Whether a district court’s fact finding that counsel was absent during a critical stage of trial should be reviewed for clear error.
