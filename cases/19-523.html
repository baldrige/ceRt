Questions presented

  1. Whether the April 3, 2014 revocation of Plaintiff’s Arkansas medical license by the Arkansas State Board of Medical Examiners (“Medical Board”) gave rise to a “new” separate and independent federal cause of action not barred by res judicata due to a civil action filed in Arkansas state court on February 25, 2014.

  2. Whether the defendants are entitled to qualified immunity instead of absolute with respect to Plaintiff’s federal claims arising from the improper revocation of his Arkansas medical license and the subsequent adverse negative reports related thereto.
