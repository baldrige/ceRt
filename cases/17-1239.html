Question presented

The South Carolina Court of Appeals affirmed the lower court’s order terminating Petitioner’s parental rights as to Minor Child. The Supreme Court of South Carolina granted a writ of certiorari in the matter but dismissed that writ as improvidently granted. The question presented is as follows: Whether it violates the Due Process Clause of the United States Constitution to terminate a person’s parental rights based on the person’s inability to explain the cause of injuries to a child.
