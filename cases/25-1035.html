Question presented

The Freedom of Information Act requires federal agencies to make information available to the public upon request unless the information falls within one of nine exemptions. Exemption 7 covers certain “records or information compiled for law enforcement purposes.” 5 U.S.C. § 552(b)(7). The question presented is: Whether “records or information compiled for law enforcement purposes” encompasses all records or information compiled by a law enforcement agency, even those not compiled for law enforcement purposes.
