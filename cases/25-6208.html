Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-6208

Matthew Peckham v. Rhode Island

IFP petition · Supreme Court of Rhode Island, No. 2023-75-C.A. · judgment July 30, 2025

Certiorari denied · March 23, 2026

Questions presented

  1. Whether the Sixth and Fourteenth Amendments permit a state court to bar all cross-examination of a key prosecution witness concerning her own juvenile arrest and case dismissal—evidence directly bearing on bias and motive to fabricate—on the ground that the records are sealed under a state confidentiality statute.

  2. Whether such a categorical denial of bias impeachment can ever be deemed harmless beyond a reasonable doubt under Chapman v. California, 386 U.S. 18 (1967), when the witness is central to the prosecution’s case and the State’s proof of guilt turns on credibility. PARTIES TO THE PROCEEDINGS Petitioner: Matthew Peckham.
