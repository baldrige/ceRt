Question presented

Whether the Court should resolve the Seventh Circuit’s departure from the summary judgment standard articulated by this Court in Anderson v. Liberty Lobby, Inc. , 477 U.S. 242 (1986), by construing facts and reasonable inferences in favor of a moving party. Whether the Court should resolve the Seventh Circuit’s newly created causation standard in Civil Rights Cases that directly conflicts with Supreme Court and Seventh Circuit precedent.
