Question presented

Questions for Review This court, the Supreme Court of the United States, granted enemy combatants the right to be heard, therefore, shouldn't we, American-born citizens of the USA, with the husband of Frances, and the father of Lawrence and Laurie, as well as the grandfather of Lawrence and Laurie, having been honest, decent, patriotic American men who sacrificed for this country, the United States of America, and served honorably in the United States military, be justly granted full and fair opportunities to be heard? Shall this Court allow any judge to abuse his or her power and illegally violate the Constitution of the United States and unconstitutionally deprive us of our right to be heard? Shall this Court allow judges to willfully, intentionally, and maliciously make false statements, refuse to disqualify themselves when served with timely, legally-sufficient motions to disqualify, and illegally not remove a judge when by law they are required to do so? Shall this Court allow judges to illegally refuse to disclose exp arte communications, interactions, and monetary transactions? Shall this court allow judges, lawyers, and defendants to obstruct justice?
