Supreme Court of the United States · No. 18-328

Kevin C. Rotkiske v. Paul Klemm, et al.

Paid petition · United States Court of Appeals for the Third Circuit, No. 16-1668 · judgment May 15, 2018

Decided · December 10, 2019
Pre-decision estimate: 5% cert probability

Question presented

Whether the "discovery rule" applies to toll the one (1) year statute of limitations under the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act, 15 U.S.C. §§ 1692, et seq., as the Fourth and Ninth Circuits have held but the Third Circuit (sua sponte en banc) has held contrarily.
