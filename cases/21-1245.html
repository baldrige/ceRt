Question presented

Whether the Federal Credit Union Act—which exempts federal credit unions “from all taxation” other than taxes on credit unions’ real property and tangible personal property, 12 U.S.C. § 1768—prohibits the imposition of a state tax on the recording of federal credit union mortgages.
