Question presented

Whether subject matter jurisdiction was properly invoked in tolling the statute of limitations in underlying claims based on fraudulent concealment, embezzlement, conversion and/or other nefarious activities. Whether a timely amended well-pled complaint and court record pleadings with attached evidence was sufficient to withstand dismissal under Rule 12(h)(6). Whether the District Court and Fifth Circuit Court of Appeals deviated from acceptable judicial proceedings in this case that require this Court's supervisory power pursuant to Rule 10. Whether procedural and substantive Due Process as guaranteed by the United States Constitution, Fourteenth Amendment and Equal Rights Clause was denied in this case.
