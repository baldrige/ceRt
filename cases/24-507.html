Question presented

Whether a public official and his Municipality have the right to discharge from employment a subordinate public employee, due to the nature of that employee’s speech as to a matter of admitted public concern, more than a year after such alleged speech took place. The City of De Soto, Missouri’s belated decision to terminate James Daly’s employment violates the Free Speech Clause of the First Amendment.
