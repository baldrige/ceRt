Question presented

Did the trial court commit reversible error’s dismissing the petition for an injunction to prevent Respondent from stalking? Did the officer violate the Petitioners 4th Amendment rights? Are the trial courts in violation of 18 U.S.C. § Section 242 Deprivation of Rights under Color of Law?
