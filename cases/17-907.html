Questions presented

  1. Whether limits on radioactive emissions measured at the point of discharge, imposed by the AEC as a license condition pursuant to its regulatory authority, give rise to an enforceable federal standard of care for purposes of the duty element of a public liability action under the Price-Anderson Act.

  2. Whether a purported state law causation standard which imposes an insurmountable burden to establish liability for injury from operation of a nuclear materials plant is inconsistent with the Price-Anderson Act.

  3. Whether a Court of Appeals may apply de novo review to overrule a District Court’s decision to allow expert testimony.
