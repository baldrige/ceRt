Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-7407

Timothy Martin v. USAA General Indemnity Company

IFP petition · United States Court of Appeals for the First Circuit, No. 25-1253 · judgment December 3, 2025

Certiorari denied · June 22, 2026

Questions presented

  1. Whether the 30-day deadline for filing a civil notice of appeal, as codified in 28 U.S.C. § 2107 and implemented by Federal Rule of Appellate Procedure 4(a)(1)(A), is categorically jurisdictional and immune from equitable tolling when delay results solely from government postal failure.

  2. Whether the rigid, no-exceptions application of Rule 4(a)(1)(A) in such circumstances violates the Due Process Clause of the Fifth Amendment by denying meaningful access to appellate review when the delay is not attributable to the litigant’s fault.
