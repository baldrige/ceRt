Questions presented

  1. Are Pro se litigants entitled to the same interpretation and protections of the law when the opposing party is a government agency as litigants represented by legal counsel.

Whether the District Court erred by dismissing Plaintiff- Appellant Donnahue George case for failure to state a claim when defendants were properly served, and Plaintiff- Appellant Donnahue George was very specific in his Amended complaint about Defendants unconstitutional actions. The Defendants entered Plaintiff Donnahue George premises illegally under the color of law and stole his personal property, thereby denying him his constitutional rights to do process and violating his constitutional rights against unjust seizure of his property.
