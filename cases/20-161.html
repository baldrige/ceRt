Questions presented

  1. Whether the First Amendment protects intimate associations absent expressive activity;

  2. If so, whether that protection exceeds what the Due Process Clause provides;

  3. Whether sibling relationships can qualify as intimate absent cohabitation; and

  4. Whether liability under 42 U.S.C. § 1983 for associational deprivation requires an intent to harm the protected association, rather than also encompassing incidental results.
