Questions presented

  1. Whether a state ofﬁcial’s demand for all signiﬁcant donors to a nonproﬁt organization, as a precondition to engaging in constitutionally-protected speech, constitutes a First Amendment injury.

  2. Whether ofﬁcial demands for membership or donor information outside the electoral context should be reviewed under strict or exacting scrutiny.
