Question presented

47 U.S.C. § 227(a)(1) of the Telephone Consumer Protection Act (“TCPA”) defines an automatic telephone dialing system (“autodialer”) as equipment which has the capacity (A) to store or produce telephone numbers to be called, using a random or sequential number generator; and (B) to dial such numbers. The question presented is as follows: Whether equipment that stores telephone numbers to be called using a random or sequential number generator and dials such numbers qualifies as an autodialer.
