Certiorari denied · October 13, 2020
Pre-decision estimate: 1% cert probability

Questions presented

  1. Whether both chambers of a state legislature, acting together, have institutional standing to sue the federal government when the federal government commandeers state funds for a federal program.

  2. Whether the federal government can constitutionally coerce a state to pay for a federal program from which the state has withdrawn by threatening to cut all the state’s Medicaid funding.
