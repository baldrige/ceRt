Question presented

Whether a record entitled Minnesota Rules of Court, Rule 220 "Birth Certificates" ("Rule 220") in due form have full faith and credit in the District of Columbia as prescribed in Article IV. Section 1. of the Constitution for the United States of America ("Const. U. S. art. 4, § 1"). Whether Respondent Ida Williams as Recorder of Deeds have a duty and obligation to record Keith Roderick Broadnax's and Rodney Ryan Graves's Certificates in the public record of the Recorder of Deeds, pursuant to Statutes at Large 66 Stat. 129, June 9, 1952; Section 1. Whether Keith and Rodney have standing under Super. Ct. R. Civ. P. 57 to seek clarification and a declaration of their substantive rights and status under their Certificates.
