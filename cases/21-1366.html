Questions presented

  1. Whether Fed. R. Civ. P. 11 requires the specific offending statements, if applicable, to be listed in the show cause order to satisfy proper notice requirements under the Due Process Clause.

  3. Whether the U. S. Supreme Court should reconcile the circuit split on how attorneys are disciplined at the federal appellate level to provide consistency and fairness across the circuits.
