Question presented

In Americold Realty Trust v. ConAgra Foods, 136 S.Ct. 1012 (2016), this Court ruled that, for diversity, a real estate investment trust was a citizen of the states for each of its members. This petition raises a related issue: When a mortgage investment trust removes a case from federal court based on diversity, is its citizenship determined by the citizenship of each of its members, or by the citizenship of its trustee?
