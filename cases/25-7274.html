Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-7274

Merry Fotso v. Col. Needham, et al.

IFP petition · Court of Appeals of Washington, Division 1, No. 87712-7-I · judgment April 17, 2025

Pending

Questions presented

  1. Whether due process is violated when a court dismisses an action with prejudice while declining to recognize documented proof of service acknowledged by opposing counsel.

  2. Whether a court may require a plaintiff to abandon a properly served defendant as a condition for proceeding with a hearing.

  3. Whether inconsistent application of procedural rules governing service of documents deprives a litigant of due process.

  4. Whether courts may dismiss a breach-of-contract claim involving an online platform by recharacterizing the claim as a privacy dispute despite the contractual language relied upon in the complaint.

  5. Whether an online platform may invoke immunity under 47 U.S.C. § 230 for content associated with a user's profile while simultaneously exercising control over that content and denying the user the ability to remove or modify it.
