Questions presented

  1. Does the 14th Amendment require a state trial court judge to inform a party of Wisconsin’s absolute right to appeal in a civil tort case at a hearing on a Motion to Dismiss and in its written order of dismissal?

  2. If so, is there in an equitable exception when the trial court merely states its dismissal was with prejudice and assumes that the party knows what that term meant?
