Question presented

Is it illegal to criticize police in North Carolina? Do citizens, using their God-given rights of life, liberty and pursuing their happiness, have the right to expect that police will respect those rights and not expect them to immediately kowtow? If police are given the "objective reasonableness" standard, shouldn't citizens, when confronted with gun carrying “public servants”, be given grace as they move from being free to being detained? Especially as it related to the growing false accusations of “swatting”?
