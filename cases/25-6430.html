Supreme Court of the United States · No. 25-6430

Jesse Michael Holliday v. California

IFP petition · Court of Appeal of California, Third Appellate District, No. C016752 · judgment October 19, 1994

Certiorari denied · February 23, 2026

Questions presented

  1. Whether the due process rights of the petitioner were violated when he was subjected to multiple pretrial assaults/batteries/maiming by deputies acting under color of judicial authority, without redress or protection from the trial court.

  2. Whether a judge who authorized or ordered multiple violent assaults on a pretrial detainee by sheriffs deputies forfeits jurisdiction to proceed over that defendant’s criminal case, or to command a separate Judge to perform in His place.

  3. Whether the refusal of state courts to review overwhelming evidence of judicial misconduct and unconstitutional treatment of a pretrial detainee constitutes a denial of due process and equal protection under state and Federal Laws.
