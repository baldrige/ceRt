Question presented

Federal law divides prisoners seeking postconviction relief into two categories: those in state custody (covered under 28 U.S.C. § 2254) and those in federal custody (covered under 28 U.S.C. § 2255). A separate provision, 28 U.S.C. § 2244(b)(1), provides that “[a] claim presented in a second or successive application under section 2254 that was presented in a prior application shall be dismissed.” The question presented is: Whether 28 U.S.C. § 2244(b)(1) applies to federal prisoners seeking relief under 28 U.S.C. § 2255.
