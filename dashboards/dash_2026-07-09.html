A. The overarching issue. Whether the Fifth Circuit erred by dismissing Ms. Hickman’s appeal based on the waiver of appeal provisions in her Plea Agreement. B. The basis for relief from this Court. After the United States Court of Appeals for Fifth Circuit dismissed Ms. Hickman’s appeal, this Court rendered its decision in Hunter v. United States, 608 U.S. ____, 2026 WL 1751815 (June 18, 2026). Hunter establishes that a defendant/appellant can challenge the enforceability of a waiver of appeal provision “when it would result in a miscarriage of justice[.]” Id. at 4.
